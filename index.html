About Me

I'm a self-driven, solutions-focused professional with a strong entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for commercial real estate.

I take pride in identifying and solving problems efficiently, maintaining professionalism, and adapting quickly to changing environments. With sharp attention to detail, I consistently meet deadlines, exceed expectations, and support team goals.

Before graduating high school, I successfully launched and operated two businesses—an experience that shaped my proactive mindset and client-first approach. I also served as a missionary in São Paulo, Brazil and Tampa, Florida, which gave me fluency in Portuguese and a deep appreciation for cross-cultural communication.
